TIPS
- Dribble, Block and Miniduels further calculate the "Winning rate" from the total form.
  As a result, it's not always the case that the player who exceeds the total form wins.
  Winning rate = The cube of Total Form / (The cube of Total Form + The cube of Opponent's Total Form).

- The total form of the shoot block (block) means "all the shots below this total form can be power down".
  "Miss" will occur if the shoot side is over this total form.

- Power down has the same effect as "decay of shoot power by distance".
  If you power down a shot that was made from the distance where the shoot power becomes 80%, it will be corrected down to 30% shoot.

- Shoot Chain is a mechanism to "recalculate as a chain unit's shoot".
  When Jude who has Prime Legend G1 chainning Samford's Emperor Penguin No.1 G5 with Prime Legend, the shoot is calculated as the Prime Legend G1.
  This is because "Prime Legend G1 is stronger than Emperor Penguin No.1 G1".
  Basically, Special Moves growth rate is not taken into consideration in the Shoot Chain.
  But, after deciding on Special Move the growth rate will be taken into account.
  When Axel who has Grand Fire G5 chainning Arachne's Grand Fire G2 with Fire Tornado,
  Grand Fire is applied to the shot.
  At this time, The growth rate of Axel side's Grand Fire is applied.
  Grand Fire will be Grand Fire G5.

- Shoot Chain will make the Total Form 1.1 times.
  This page's calcurate is Case by when chainning once.
  Case by when chainning twice, please total form will be multiplied by 1.1.

- Offense Plus and Defense Plus will activate 2 to 5 people depending on the position of formation.
  Those with these skills are not necessarily strengthened.

- The position to activate Shoot Plus is unknown.
  We want your ideas.
  It corresponded. (ver 1.4.)

- FW can use to "Distance effect" to gain a victory.
  Let's as close to the center of the opponent's goal as possible.

- "Put Your Back Into It!" is deactivated when FP is less than 10.

- The gauge set with normal shoot does not affect the Special Move Shoot.

- When shot, the scale is displayed if you don't use the Special Move.
  That scale's effect is "Raising the Total Form".
  Two scales are 1.1 times, three scales are 1.2 times, and four scales are 1.3 times.

- In Situation when Special Move is not selected.
  The fluctuation range of the total form does not change regardless of which of the left and right display items is selected.
